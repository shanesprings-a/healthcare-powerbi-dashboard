# 📊 Healthcare Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

---

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents a multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing healthcare data, focusing on patient demographics, billing trends, and length of stay.

It demonstrates how data visualization and modeling can be used to uncover insights and support data-driven decision-making in a healthcare setting.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze healthcare data and identify trends in patient distribution, admission types, medical conditions, and cost patterns.

---

## 🧰 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query (data transformation)
- DAX (calculated measures)
- Data visualization best practices

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Insurance & Admissions
- Average billing by insurance provider  
- Patient distribution by admission type  
- Key KPIs (average billing, average stay, patient count)  

### 🔹 Demographics
- Gender distribution across age groups  
- Patient distribution by age group  
- Comparison of billing and length of stay  

### 🔹 Cost vs Length of Stay
- Relationship between cost and patient stay duration  
- Breakdown by medical condition  
- Patient stay category distribution  

### 🔹 Patient & Condition Overview
- Billing trends by medical condition  
- Gender-based comparisons  
- Cross-filtering across all visuals  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Older age groups show higher patient volumes  
- Billing is relatively consistent across insurance providers  
- Length of stay is a major driver of total cost  
- Certain conditions show higher cost per day  

---

## 🎛️ Interactivity
- Dynamic slicers for:
  - Insurance provider  
  - Admission type  
  - Test results  
  - Age group  
  - Medical condition
